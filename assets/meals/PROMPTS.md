## Shared production prompt

> Reusable food card image for a Japanese-inspired personal planner app. Minimal warm off-white washi-paper-like tabletop, no props except a subtle black sumi-e brush accent at one edge. Premium natural editorial food photography with realistic texture. Overhead close shot, centered handmade warm off-white ceramic plate or bowl, all food fully visible, landscape-friendly crop. Soft diffused daylight, calm disciplined Japanese minimalism. Off-white and ink-black base with natural food colors and one restrained ember-red accent. No text, logo, watermark, chopsticks, extra dishes, or dark background.
